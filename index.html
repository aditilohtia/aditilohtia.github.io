About Me

Hello! My name is Aditi Lohtia and I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Currently, I am working for the Ontario Ministry of the Attorney General, Office of the Public Guardian and Trustee as a System Support Officer.

I have had an interest in technology from a young age, even creating my first website at age 10 (check it out!). I hope to pursue a career involving business and technology, with emphasis in finance. I also have interests in science and environment, and how computers can help revolutionize our ever-changing world.

My hobbies include tennis and dance, with 8 years of training in Indian Classical Dance. I enjoy travelling and hope to explore more of the world through my co-op experiences. I am an avid social media user, and have previously hosted a fashion page on Instagram, collaborating with several clothing companies.

I have lived in both Canada and the United States, near big cities including Toronto, Manhatten, and Philadelphia!
